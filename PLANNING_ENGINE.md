Multi-Objective Optimization
The Planner evaluates and scores candidate actions against a weighted matrix of active objectives instead of generating text.

2. The Objective Hierarchy

System Objectives (Weight: 1.0): Invariant constitutional rules, such as preserving dignity and doing no harm.


User Objectives (Weight: 0.8): Long-term life goals, like maintaining independence or walking daily.


Session Objectives (Weight: 0.5): Immediate conversational goals, such as confirming medication.
